Netaji Subhas University

Netaji Subhas University (NSU), Jamshedpur is a leading private university in Jharkhand,
committed to academic excellence, innovation, and social responsibility. Established under
the Jharkhand State Private University Act, 2018, and recognized by the UGC (Section 2(f) of
the UGC Act, 1956), NSU offers globally-aligned education in a vibrant learning environment.
Inspired by the legacy of Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose, NSU upholds values of courage, discipline,
and nation-building. The university provides a wide spectrum of diploma, undergraduate, postgraduate
and PhD programs across disciplines such as Engineering, Management, Law, IT, Science, Education, Nursing,
Pharmacy, Commerce, Arts, and Mass Communication.NSU,s programs are approved by key regulatory bodies
including AICTE, NCTE, BCI, PCI, INC, and JNRC, ensuring top-tier academic standards.With a lush, modern campus,
experienced faculty, and state-of-the-art infrastructure, NSU emphasizes research, experiential learnin
and holistic student development. From tech labs and business incubators to cultural clubs and communit
outreach, NSU nurtures both academic and personal growth.
As a rising name in Indian higher education, NSU continues to expand its reach with future-ready courses and
global collaborations, preparing students to thrive in a dynamic world.
